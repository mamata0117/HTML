Death note is a Japanese manga series written by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata.Its main characters are Light Yagami and L.This showcase the story of a student who finds a supernatural notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name is written in it. As the story progresses, Light becomes increasingly obsessed with his own power and the idea of becoming a god.L cames to investigate the case.They showcase the surreal and psychological elements of the story.
